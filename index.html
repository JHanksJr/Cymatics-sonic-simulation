About Cymatics & Flower of Life

Cymatics reveals how sound creates geometric patterns in nature. The Flower of Life pattern shows how different frequencies generate increasing complexity in sacred geometry - from a single circle at low frequencies to intricate multi-layered mandalas at higher frequencies. As you increase the frequency, more circles are added following the ancient Flower of Life progression: Seed of Life (7 circles), Flower of Life (19 circles), and beyond into complex sacred geometric forms that appear throughout nature and spiritual traditions.
